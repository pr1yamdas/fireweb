Fire Safety Tips

Here are some essential fire safety tips:

  • Install smoke detectors in key areas of your home or building.
  • Have a fire evacuation plan and practice it regularly.
  • Keep fire extinguishers accessible and ensure everyone knows how to use them.
  • Do not overload electrical outlets and ensure wiring is in good condition.
  • Close doors behind you when exiting a burning room to slow down the spread of fire and smoke.
  • Stay low to the ground in case of smoke to minimize inhalation of toxic fumes.
